So You Want To Be a Chipmunk!

December 2007

Manhattan Place Entertainment in association with Nickelodeon Movies and DreamWorks Pictures recently produced a: 30 second commercial spot in connection with the upcoming release of the "Alvin and the Chipmunks" movie.

The spot was shot and directed by MPE's Steve Cohen in Times Square in New York City.

This is the 4th project in the past 3 months that Manhattan Place Entertainment has been selected as the production company of choice by Nickelodeon Movies and DreamWorks Pictures.

The previous projects included a music video for Jerry Seinfeld's Bee Movie and two spots for the upcoming Spiderwick Chronicles.
