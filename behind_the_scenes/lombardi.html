"Lombardi" delivers stirring Pre-Game speech to Packers

Dan Lauria, the star of Broadway’s hit play "Lombardi" performed a compelling Super Bowl pre-game speech that addressed the reigning NFC champion Green Bay Packers, imploring them them to play their best in the Super Bowl.

MPE’s Steve Cohen shot and directed both features that aired during the FOX Sports coverage of Super Bowl XLV.
